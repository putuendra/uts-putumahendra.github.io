About Us

Sangeh Park is located in the village of Sangeh, Badung Regency, 25 km from Denpasar City. The status of this area was previously a nature reserve and with the issuance of the Decree of the Minister of Forestry No. 87 / Kps-II / 1993 dated 16 February 1993 turned into a Nature Tourism Park with a total area of ??13,969 ha. Sangeh Nature Tourism Object is not only famous for its tame kerf, but because of the 10ha. Homogeneous Nutmeg Forest (Dipterocarpus trinervis), the seeds are inedible and also the Bukit Sari Temple which is a heritage temple of the 17th century at the time of the heyday of the Mengwi kingdom.
